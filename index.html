The beginning

  • Of the 50 to 200 eggs laid, roughly 20% will never hatch
  • ~ 45 days later, baby sea turtles begin their journey to the sea
  • Their size is about 2 inches to relatively close to an adult hand

The Challenges!

  • Black Vulture / Crab / Sea Gull / ...
  • And many other threats , including human waste, takes out roughly around 50% of the hatchlings
